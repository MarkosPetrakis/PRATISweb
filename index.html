About Pratis

At Pratis OE, we're more than just a business; we're a family. Founded on the principles of integrity, dedication, and genuine care, our company was built from the ground up with a single, clear mission: to treat every customer like a part of our family. As a family-run business, our name and reputation are at the heart of everything we do. We believe that exceptional service and fair pricing aren't just business strategies—they are the cornerstones of a lasting relationship. In a market often dominated by large, impersonal corporations, we stand out by offering a personal touch. When you contact us, you're speaking with someone who is personally invested in your success and satisfaction.

Our Featured Brands

ROSAGRES

ROSAGRES is known for its high-quality porcelain stoneware products for swimming pools, outdoor/industrial pavements, and high-traffic areas. The company is a leader in the swimming pool sector, particularly public pools, and is recognized for its innovative and durable solutions, including specialized overflow systems.

SUPERGRES

Supergres is an Italian company known for producing high-quality, innovative porcelain stoneware tiles and white body wall tiles for residential and commercial spaces, recognized for their realistic Italian design, sophisticated finishes, and versatile aesthetic appeal in styles ranging from classic to modern.

Quintessenza Ceramiche

Quintessenza Ceramiche is known for its innovative and high-quality small-format ceramic tiles, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern technology, and focusing on unique design, sophisticated color palettes, and textures.
